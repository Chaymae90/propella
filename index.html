About us

Who are we ?

Propella is a collective of founders and startup professionals who provide you with the support to build your business.

We are founders, executives and professionals from a wide range of backgrounds and experience building start-up businesses.

We have expertise across a broad range of sectors and work with our own experienced teams. Our members choose to work with projects they believe in, so you’ll find a motivated partner for your team.

Why we're here

New Founders who start off on the founding journey can find valuable advice from people who’ve been on the same journey.

Founders themselves love to work outside of areas in their own business or to assist other founders.

We have experience operating in markets around the world and we operate a truly remote team. We’re based out of London, a city with access to some of the best minds and largest investors in the world.
